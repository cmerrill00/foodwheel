      Help & Features

      The Wheel

      The wheel randomly selects a meal from your list when you click Spin the Wheel. The yellow triangle at the top is the pointer — whatever it lands on is the result. The button stays disabled until you have at least two options added. If a category or feature filter is active, only the filtered options appear on the wheel.

      Adding Locations

      Click + Add Location in the Food Options header to open the entry form. Available fields:

      • Name — the restaurant or meal name (required)
      • Category — an optional label for grouping, such as "Fast Food" or "Italian"
      • Website URL — if provided, the name becomes a clickable link in your list; you can enter the URL with or without https://
      • Drive-thru — check if the location has a drive-thru
      • Online ordering — check if online ordering is available
      • Dine-in only — check if dine-in is the only option

      Press Add Location to save or Cancel to close without saving.

      Managing Your List

      Each entry shows three icons on the right side:

      • 📝 — Opens the notes panel for that location. The icon turns gold when notes have been saved.
      • ✏️ — Opens an inline editor for the name, category, URL, and all feature flags. Press Enter or the save icon to confirm, or ✕ to cancel.
      • ✕ — Permanently removes the entry from your list.

      Notes

      Click the 📝 icon on any entry to open its notes panel. Use it to record favorite dishes, past experiences, or anything that might influence your next decision. Notes are saved per location and persist across sessions. Click outside the panel, press Escape, or use the Cancel button to close without saving.

      Filtering the Wheel

      Two rows of filter chips appear above the wheel once you have categories or feature flags set:

      • Row 1 — Categories: Tap one or more category chips to limit the wheel to those groups. Multiple categories can be active at once (results from any selected category are included). Tap All to clear the category filter.
      • Row 2 — Features: Tap 🚗 Drive-thru, 📱 Online ordering, or 🍽️ Dine-in only to filter by those attributes. Only locations that match all active feature filters are included. These chips only appear when at least one entry has that feature enabled.

      Category and feature filters combine — for example, selecting "Fast Food" and 🚗 Drive-thru shows only Fast Food locations that also have drive-thru.

      Pick History

      Every spin is automatically logged in the Pick History section with the date and time. From there you can:

      • Check the checkbox next to an entry to mark it as eaten. Confirmed entries are visually struck through and are the only ones counted by the weighting system.
      • Use the Mark as Eaten button that appears directly below the spin result to confirm quickly without scrolling down.
      • Click ✕ on any history entry to remove it individually.
      • Use Clear History in the ⚙ settings panel to wipe all entries at once.

      Reducing Recent Repeats (Weighting)

      Open the ⚙ panel and enable Reduce chance of recently eaten foods. Two values control how it works:

      • Reduce by X% — how much to lower the probability for a location that was recently confirmed eaten. A value of 50% means that location is half as likely to be picked.
      • Within the last X days — how far back to look in history. Only confirmed (checked) entries within this window are counted.

      When weighting is active, slice sizes on the wheel visually reflect the adjusted probabilities — a reduced location gets a narrower slice. Click Save to apply changes.

      Backup & Restore

      All backup and restore options are in the ⚙ settings panel under Backup & Restore:

      • Export Config — Downloads a food-wheel-config.json file containing your full food list and settings. Pick history is not included.
      • Import Config — Load a previously exported config file. This replaces your current list and settings.
      • Load Defaults — Replaces your list with a built-in set of common US restaurant chains, pre-filled with categories, websites, and feature flags.
      • Clear Config — Wipes your entire food list and resets settings to defaults. You will be offered a backup download before the data is cleared.
      • Clear History — Removes all entries from Pick History. Food options and settings are not affected.

      Import, Load Defaults, and Clear Config all ask for confirmation before making changes.
